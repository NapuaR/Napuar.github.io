The target audience includes men and woman of the ages of eighteen and above who are either looking to plan their next holiday, wanting to go out on an adventure the next day or wish to know the current state of the weather conditions outside.

Meet Fred and Jane

Fred

Fred is a middle-aged man of 35, is the owner of his own company and thus has a busy work schedule and is always on the go. Because of this he spends all his time browsing on his mobile phone. He hates carrying around extra layers, loves to be dressed just for the weather. As such when he visits "weatherme.us" he expects to see today's current weather coditions and any changes that may take place so he can plan accordingly. He values strong communication, timely reports, and a clear fulfillment of expectations. Orderly, timely and a well-respected office man, this is Fred.

Jane

Jane is a sweet girl of 18, she love fun and adventure and is an avid hiker in her local community. She plays softball, values her friends and family. She works as a waitress and saving up to go to college in the next year. When it comes to checking the weather she also uses her phone a lot but does check it at home on her laptop just in case things have changed. She is in no rush as her life won't be picking up unitl her first year of college. Fun, sweet, relaxed and planning various hiking trips outside, this is Jane.

Scenarios

  1. Fred is off to a busy day at the office He knows that being in the height of spring, the weather changes on a dime. A little frustrated he checks his phone for today's the weather wanting to get some results fast so he can get on with his day.

  2. Jane is planning a weeknd away with her friends, they have plans to go hiking, camping and fishing. Jane wants to make sure this weekend will be the best weeknd for the trip, she checks her phone while out buying supplies and re-checks it at home on her laptop just to make sure their plans won't be spoiled.

  3. Fred has a very important meeting with a client hearing the client's love for the outdoors Fred has scheduled it outside in attempt to be on the good side. Fred wants to make sure mother nature won't sent a nasty on the day of his big meeting, he therefore checks the weather on his phone and confirms the date with the client.

  4. Jane has just finished a long shift at work and wishes to get away for a few hours, she quickly checks her phone on "weatherme.us" and is pleased to find fair conditions for the next few hours.

  5. Fred has come to the end of his week, being so busy throughout the week doesn't leave him much time to take his dog "Jake" out for a nice walk. Coming upon a Saturday morning, Fred locates he's favorite site on his laptop and is pleased to find that the weather is in fine shape for his outing with "Jake" the dog.
